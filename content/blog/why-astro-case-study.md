This project paired Decap CMS with Astro's content collections so PMs could publish merchandising notes faster than ops could refresh dashboards.

Highlights:

- Markdown MDX islands gave writers custom callouts without bundling React.
- Streaming `content` collections into cards kept bundle cost under 40kb.
- Edge deployment meant APAC shoppers actually saw targeted promos.
